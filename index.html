The structure of this site

There is 3 main div. There were .header, .hero, .features
.header contains h1 and ul . there is a space between h1 and ul . There is three element of li in ul . every li contains an a.
in .hero there is .hero-inner. .hero enables .hero-inner placed in center by display:flex; justify-content:center
.features contain .features-inner . Grid display is set in .features-inner
